Search Subject

To search for a subject:
 

  1. Click on the Search By: drop down list and select Last Name, First Name or Identifier.
  2. Enter the Last Name, First Name or Identifier in the Search Criteria field and click the Search button. Note: You can also leave the Search Criteria field blank and click Search to list all studies in the system.
  3. Search results will appear in the bottom of the page. Click on a subject to view the summary profile.
