Study Sites

All fields with a red asterisk * are required.

 

To enter the study site, enter the first few letters of the name of the Organization conducting the trial and select it from the drop down list that appears. If this is a Multi-Institutional: study, enter all the Organization sites for the study on this page.

 

  1. Enter the Organization conducting the trial. This is a prepopulated field. Enter the first few letters of the name of the Disease Catego
  2. ry and select it from the drop down list that appears. Note: If you are entering a multi-site study, click Add Organization and enter the name of another site. Repeat until all sites for the study have been added.
  3. Enter the Activation Date (mm/dd/yyyy). You can also click on the Calendar icon to select a date. This is the date that a site begins participation in a Study.
  4. Enter the IRB Approval Date (mm/dd/yyyy). You can also click on the Calendar icon to select a date. This is the date that the institutional review board or research ethics board authorized approval or most recent approval of the Study protocol for that site.
  5. Enter the Target Accrual Number. This is the total number of patients/subjects/participants needed for protocol enrollment at this site.
  6. Click Save or Save & Continue to go to the next task page.
