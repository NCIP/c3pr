Manage Study

To search for a study:
 

  1. Click on the Search By: drop down menu and select an option .
  2. Enter the Identifier, Short Title or Status in the Search Criteria field and click the Search button. Note: You can also leave the Search Criteria field blank and click Search to list all studies in the system.
  3. Search results will appear in the bottom of the page. Click on a study to open it.

 

Once the Study has been selected you can (1) view the registration, (2) edit the enrollment details, (3)print the Study, (4) export the Study, or (5) change its current epoch.

To view the study:

  1. Click on a study to select it from the search results. C3PR displays the Summary page.
  2. You can change the registration status by clicking the Change Status button. Click on the Edit button under the Sites section to edit the site status.
To edit the study:
  1. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Edit Study button.
To amend the study:
  1. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Amend Study button.
 

To print the study:

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Print button.

To export the study:

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Export Study button.
