Stratification

All fields with a red asterisk * are required.

 

  1. Click Add Stratification Factor and enter the Stratification Question and Answers. This is a specific Question to be answered in order to identify the stratum group for the Study subject. Enter at least two Answers the subject can choose from. Click the Add Answer button to create additional answers. Note: these are required fields. Click on the to remove both the question and answer.
  2. Click the Generate Stratum Groups button to generate the stratum groups.
  3. Click Save or Save & Continue to go to the next task page.
