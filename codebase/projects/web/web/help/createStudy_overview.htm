Overview

All fields with a red asterisk * are required.
 

Complete the Overview task page:
  1. Review the Basic Details
  2. Review the Sites
  3. Review the Identifiers
  4. Review the Investigator
  5. Review the Personnel
  6. Review the Stratification Factor
  7. Review the Stratum Groups
  8. Review the Diseases
  9. Review the Epochs & Arms
  10. Review the Notifications
  11. At the bottom of the Overview task page there are three options:
    1. Click on Save to save the Study information. A confirmation page will display that the Study successfully created. Note: C3PR will save the data entries but the Study may not be active.
    2. Click on Save and Activate to save and active the Study. A confirmation page will display that the Study successfully created. Note: A Study can only be activated if all requirements are met.
    3. Click on Print to print the Study Overview.
