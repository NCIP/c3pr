Cancer Central Clinical Participant Registry (C3PR)

C3PR is a web-based application used for end-to-end registration of patients to clinical trials. This includes capturing the consent signed date, eligibility criteria, stratification, randomization, and screening. Clinical workflows are enabled by both subject- and study-centric views into the registration process.
Note: The C3PR Administrator must first enter information for Organizations, Subjects, Investigators and Research Staff before end users can enter and save information in the system.

C3PR Administrators

 There are pre-populated fields in the C3PR application that first need to be configured by the administrator. The following must be pre-defined and entered into C3PR by the administrator before end users can enter and save information in the system.

  • Study Organizations (including the names of the Coordinating Centers)
  • Subjects (including the Organization Assigning Authorities)
  • Principal Investigators
  • Research Staff

If any of the steps above have not been completed by the C3PR system administrator, a study cannot be created.
 

Note: Study Management is not the primary function of C3PR. However, Study Management is critical for the independent functioning of C3PR because registration is driven by the structure of the study. Therefore, it is included as a function of C3PR.
