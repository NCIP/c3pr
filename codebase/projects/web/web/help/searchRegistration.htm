Search Registration

To search for a registration:
 

  1. Select Subject, Study, or Registration Identifier from the Search By drop down menu.
  2. Enter Search Criteria. This is a pre-populated field. Enter the first few letters of the search term and select an option from the drop down menu that appears.
  3. Click the Search button.
  4. If C3PR finds its match it displays the results. Click on a study in the search results to view and/or edit it.

Once the Study has been selected you can (1) view the registration, (2) edit the enrollment details, (3)print the Study, (4) export the Study, or (5) change its current epoch.

To view the registration:
 

  1. Click on a study to select it from the search results. C3PR displays the Overview page.
  2. You can edit the enrollment details if the registration status is Registered. Click on the Edit button under the Enrollment Details section. If the registration status is Take subject off study you cannot edit the registration.

To take a subject off the study:

  1. Click the Take subject off study button. Note: The Take subject off study button will only appear under the Enrollment Details heading if the subject has a status of Registered in the study.
  2. Enter the Reason.
  3. Enter the Date.
  4. Click the ok button.

To print the registration:

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Print button.

To export the registration:

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Export button.

To change the current epoch:

  1. Mouse over the Related Tasks tab in the left hand side of the page and click on Change Current Epoch in the menu that appears.
  2. C3PR displays all related epoch treatments to this registration. Drag and drop the subject to the appropriate epoch.
